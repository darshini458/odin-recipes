Chow Mein Recipe

Ingredients

For the Noodles:

  • Chow Mein noodles or egg noodles: 200 grams
  • Water: for boiling
  • Salt: 1 teaspoon

For the Stir-Fry:

  • Oil: 2 tablespoons (vegetable or sesame)
  • Garlic: 3 cloves, minced
  • Ginger: 1-inch piece, minced
  • Onion: 1 medium, thinly sliced
  • Bell peppers (red and green): 1 each, thinly sliced
  • Carrot: 1 large, julienned
  • Cabbage: 1 cup, shredded
  • Snow peas: 1/2 cup (optional)
  • Bean sprouts: 1/2 cup (optional)
  • Green onions: 2, chopped

For the Sauce:

  • Soy sauce: 3 tablespoons
  • Oyster sauce: 2 tablespoons (optional)
  • Hoisin sauce: 1 tablespoon (optional)
  • Sesame oil: 1 teaspoon
  • Sugar: 1 teaspoon
  • Black pepper: 1/2 teaspoon

Optional Ingredients:

  • Cooked chicken, beef, or shrimp: 200 grams, thinly sliced
  • Chili flakes: to taste (for spice)

Preparation Steps:

  1. Cook the noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large wok or skillet, heat the oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the garlic and ginger, and stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Add the onions and stir-fry until they become translucent.
  5. Add the bell peppers, carrot, and cabbage, and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  6. If using, add the snow peas and bean sprouts, and cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  7. Add the cooked noodles to the pan and toss to combine with the vegetables.
  8. In a small bowl, mix the soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, sesame oil, sugar, and black pepper.
  9. Pour the sauce over the noodles and vegetables, and stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until everything is well coated and heated through.
  10. Add the green onions and optional protein (if using), and toss to combine.
  11. Serve hot, garnished with additional green onions and chili flakes if desired.

Kebab Recipe

Ingredients

For the Kebabs:

  • Ground meat (beef, lamb, or chicken): 500 grams
  • Onion: 1 large, finely chopped
  • Garlic: 3 cloves, minced
  • Ginger: 1-inch piece, minced
  • Green chili: 2, finely chopped
  • Fresh coriander leaves: 1/4 cup, finely chopped
  • Fresh mint leaves: 1/4 cup, finely chopped
  • Ground cumin: 1 teaspoon
  • Ground coriander: 1 teaspoon
  • Garam masala: 1 teaspoon
  • Red chili powder: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Turmeric powder: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt: to taste
  • Lemon juice: 1 tablespoon
  • Oil or melted butter: for brushing

Optional Ingredients:

  • Skewers: if using ground meat, soak wooden skewers in water for 30 minutes to prevent burning
  • Chaat masala: for sprinkling
  • Lemon wedges: for serving
  • Yogurt sauce: for serving

Preparation Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground meat, onion, garlic, ginger, green chili, coriander leaves, mint leaves, ground cumin, ground coriander, garam masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder, salt, and lemon juice. Mix well until all ingredients are thoroughly combined.
  2. Cover the bowl and let the mixture marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes, or overnight for better flavor.
  3. If using skewers, take a small portion of the meat mixture and shape it around the skewer, pressing gently to form an even kebab. Repeat with the remaining mixture.
  4. Preheat the grill or oven to medium-high heat. If using a grill, brush the grates with oil to prevent sticking.
  5. Brush the kebabs with oil or melted butter and place them on the grill or in the oven.
  6. Cook the kebabs for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are evenly browned and cooked through.
  7. Remove the kebabs from the grill or oven and let them rest for a few minutes.
  8. Sprinkle with chaat masala, if desired, and serve with lemon wedges and yogurt sauce.
